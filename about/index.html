Who We Are

ABOUT TALLAPOOSA PAINTBALL

Nestled deep in the natural woods of Tallapoosa, Georgia, our battlefield offers an authentic and dynamic outdoor experience that challenges players of all skill levels.

We deliver top-tier gear with every rental. Cleaned, maintained, and upgraded regularly — high-performance markers and protective gear that fits right and works hard.

And with our 100% satisfaction guarantee, you can play with total confidence. Whether it's your first time or your fiftieth, you'll feel the Tallapoosa difference the moment you step onto the field.
